Dhage's English Academy

Founded in the year 2001, Dhage's English Academy is an institute that aids in the development and refinement of one's English communication skills. Over the years, we have trained countless citizens belonging to different walks of life; from students to working professionals. With years of experience in this field under our belt, we have established a repute for our quality teaching methods that help understand the specific requirements of every learner at our institute.
